ABOUT US

SINCE 1960!


The foundation of MSC Legal finds its pillars grounded deep in the history of time. The genesis of the firm can be traced back to the year 1960, the year when it first started as an office under the name Manbir Singh & Company. Through years of serving clients with excellent legal services, MSC Legal has evolved into a multi-disciplinary law office offering myriad of legal solutions in a vast variety of legal fields.

Our services in the legal field are a result of ritualistic adherence to our core values and principles - efficiency, excellence and reliability. These have allowed us to develop and maintain a client centric framework which caters to all kinds of needs with care and attention.

We take pride in having evolved into a firm which takes a systematic and a pragmatic approach to all problems and queries; a unique solution for every situation crafted with artful creativity and framed with success in mind.

The priorities of the firm are in order and a purpose is decided for. As officers of the court, we adhere to the law and believe truly in the ideals of justice and equity. As advocates, we endeavour to provide the best of legal services to preserve and sustain these ideals.
